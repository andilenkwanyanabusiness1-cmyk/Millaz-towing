About Millaz Towing

Your KZN Neighbours, Ready to Help

Who We Are

We're a family-run towing and roadside assistance company serving Pinetown, Westville, Hillcrest, Kloof, and the greater Durban area. Our team of five dedicated drivers operates a fleet of nine specialized vehicles, covering everything from compact cars to motorcycles.

As a proud member of the National African Towing Association (NATA) and approved by major insurance panels across South Africa, we combine small-business heart with professional standards. When you call Millaz Towing, you're calling neighbours who genuinely care about getting you safely back on the road.
